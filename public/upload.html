How GLP Conformity levels are defined
GLP Conformity reflects the completeness and internal consistency of documented GLP practices.
GLP initiation phase — Hatchling
Interpretation
GLP structures are at an early stage. Documentation, SOPs, and traceability practices are being introduced, but are not yet consistently applied.
Guidance
Establish core SOPs and begin structured documentation of experiments and materials.
GLP emerging — Fledgling
Interpretation
Initial GLP elements are in place. Documentation practices exist, and selected SOPs, records, or inventories are being used, but coverage and consistency remain limited.
Guidance
Expand SOP coverage and ensure regular use across ongoing experiments.
GLP in development — Owl
Interpretation
Your documentation practices are developing. SOPs and experimental records are being established, with initial inventory tracking and structured workflows in place.
Guidance
Continue linking experiments to approved SOPs to strengthen evidence alignment.
GLP consolidated — Eagle
Interpretation
GLP practices are well established. SOPs, experimental records, training, and inventory systems are consistently applied, with clear traceability across workflows.
Guidance
Maintain consistency and regularly review documentation quality and approvals.
GLP fully integrated — Dragon
Interpretation
GLP is fully integrated into daily laboratory operations. Documentation, approvals, training, and traceability are comprehensive, auditable, and embedded across all activities.
Guidance
Sustain GLP practices and support external audits, collaborations, or regulatory alignment.
